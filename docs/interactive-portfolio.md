## Procedural Generation
The gallery will be procedurally generated based on the categories you choose to display.

Why opt for procedural generation? To begin with, procedural generation adds a unique and engaging element, ensuring that every visit to the gallery offers a distinct experience.

Furthermore, it offers exceptional scalability. When the need arises to include a new project in the gallery, the generation scripts will seamlessly handle the task, expediting the process of adding new rooms.
